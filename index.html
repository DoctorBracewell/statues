Important Information!

Welcome to DrBracewell's BodyPillow Generator™. If you have ever wanted an in-game model of player, you're in the right place!

Just follow the instructions below to generate a BodyPillow ingame:

  • Upload a classic-arm-style (4px arm width) below.
  • Give your pillow a name (or not). Colour codes are supported with the & symbol
  • Hit generate, and be patient! Due to rate limits, it will take minimum of 2m to generate your pillow.
  • If you get an error, something might be messing up with the skin API. Give it a while and try again.
  • When you get your command, just head ingame and paste it into a command block! If you want one on the build server and don't have CMB access, ask a CMD to input the command and power the command block.
  • Your BodyPillow will unfortunately not have 3D layers from the original skin - any layers on the original will be pasted onto the inner layer.
